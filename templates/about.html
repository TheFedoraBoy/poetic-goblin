Poetic Goblin
Where Fantasy Lives

Our Mission

Poetic Goblin is a social platform built for fantasy fans, tabletop roleplayers, and Dungeons & Dragons enthusiasts. We believe the best stories aren't written alone — they're forged at the table, between friends, one dice roll at a time.

Our mission is to connect players, storytellers, and worldbuilders into a living community where campaigns are shared, characters come alive, and an entire world grows through play.

The World of Elysal

At the heart of Poetic Goblin is Elysal — an open-source fantasy world spanning eight ages and thousands of years of history. Elysal isn't a static setting; it's a living tapestry woven by its community.

When you play a campaign and share it here, your stories become part of the Annals — a growing chronicle of Elysal's history. Every adventure adds a thread. Every character leaves a mark. The world is built by the players who inhabit it.

What You Can Do

Forge Characters

Create characters with unique traits, backstories, and pixel avatars. Switch between them at will.

Share Campaigns

Document your tabletop sessions with multi-part campaign logs. Tag locations and players from Elysal.

Write Stories

Craft tales set in Elysal or your own worlds. Share fiction, lore entries, and character journals.

Post Art & Maps

Share illustrations, maps, tokens, and visual creations with the community.

Explore the Annals

Browse 8,000 years of lore across eight ages. Contribute your own stories to the chronicle.

Connect & Collaborate

Follow other adventurers, exchange messages, and find your next party.

Open Source World

Elysal is designed to be open and community-driven. The lore, locations, and timeline serve as a shared foundation that any group can build upon. Run a campaign in the Verdant Reaches? Share it. Invent a new faction in the Iron Wastes? Write it into the Annals. Your table's stories become part of something bigger.

We believe fantasy worlds shouldn't be locked behind a single author's vision. The best settings are the ones that grow with their communities — messy, surprising, and alive.

Support Poetic Goblin

Poetic Goblin is a passion project built and maintained by a small team of fantasy lovers. There are no ads, no premium tiers, and no paywalls — just a community-driven platform for the stories that matter to us. If you enjoy what we're building and want to help keep the torches lit, consider tossing a coin to your goblin. Every contribution goes directly toward hosting, development, and keeping Elysal alive for everyone.
